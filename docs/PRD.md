# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Shantanu Jagirdar Portfolio

## 1. Executive Summary
A premium, high-performance digital portfolio for Shantanu Jagirdar, a concert pianist, session keyboardist, and DJ. The application serves as a central hub for booking inquiries, performance schedules, and media showcasing.

## 2. Target Audience
- Event Organizers & Promoters
- Fellow Musicians & Producers
- Fans and General Public
- Wedding & Corporate Event Planners

## 3. User Stories
- **As a promoter**, I want to see a list of upcoming gigs so I can check availability and current tour status.
- **As a producer**, I want to listen to high-quality audio demos to understand the artist's style and technical proficiency.
- **As a fan**, I want to view high-quality visuals of performances to experience the artist's stage presence.
- **As a potential client**, I want a quick and elegant way to get in touch for session work or bookings.

## 4. Functional Requirements
### 4.1 Navigation
- Sticky, scroll-aware navbar.
- Mobile-first responsive drawer menu (80% width).
- Smooth anchor link scrolling.

### 4.2 Media Showcase
- **Audio Player**: Custom-styled player for listening to demos.
- **Visuals Gallery**: Grid-based display of performance photography and videos.

### 4.3 Gig Calendar
- List of upcoming performances with date, venue, and location.
- "Get Tickets" or "Inquiry" call-to-action for each event.

### 4.4 Contact & Lead Generation
- Integrated contact form.
- Direct links to WhatsApp and Instagram.
- Display of professional contact details.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance**: Fast loading of high-resolution images.
- **Design**: "Piano-inspired" aesthetic (Ivory, Ebony, Gold).
- **Responsiveness**: Flawless experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and intuitive navigation.

## 6. Success Metrics
- Increase in direct booking inquiries via the contact form.
- High engagement rate on the audio demo player.
- Low bounce rate due to premium visual design.
